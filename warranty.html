Warranty & Service Terms

Transparent terms for all our repair services

Repair Warranty

Standard Warranty: 90 days on all repair work and parts used

Coverage: Warranty covers the specific repair performed and parts replaced

Exclusions: Warranty does not cover:

  • Physical damage caused by customer after repair
  • Water damage occurring after repair
  • Software issues unrelated to the repair
  • Pre-existing conditions not addressed in the repair

Service Times

Standard Repairs: 1-3 business days (depending on parts availability)

Same Day Service: Available when parts are in stock and repair is straightforward

Complex Repairs: May take longer due to parts ordering or technical complexity

Data Recovery: Time varies based on damage severity (1-7 days typically)

Parts & Quality

Quality Parts: We use high-quality, compatible parts for all repairs

OEM Parts: Original equipment manufacturer parts when available and requested

Compatible Parts: High-quality compatible alternatives when OEM parts are unavailable

Part Sourcing: We source parts from reputable suppliers to ensure quality and reliability

Customer Responsibilities

Data Backup: Customers are responsible for backing up their data before repair

Device Condition: Please inform us of any existing damage or previous repairs

Collection: Devices should be collected within 30 days of completion

Payment: Payment is due upon collection or completion of repair
