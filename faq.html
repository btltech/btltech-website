Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, repairs, and policies.

Repairs & Timing

How long do repairs take?

Repair times vary depending on the issue and parts availability. Many common repairs, like screen or battery replacements, can often be completed the same day if we have the parts in stock. More complex issues may take 1-3 business days. We will always provide an estimated timeframe when you book your device in.

Do you offer a warranty on your repairs?

Yes, we offer a 90-day warranty on all our repairs. This warranty covers the specific parts we replaced and the labour associated with the repair. It does not cover accidental damage (like drops or water damage) that occurs after the repair. For more details, please see our Warranty Terms.

What types of devices do you repair?

We repair a wide range of electronic devices, including:

  • Smartphones (iPhone, Samsung, Google Pixel, etc.)
  • Laptops (MacBook, Dell, HP, Lenovo, etc.)
  • Tablets (iPad, Samsung Galaxy Tab, etc.)
  • Gaming Consoles (PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch)

If you have a device not listed here, please contact us to see if we can help.

Do I need to book an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend booking an appointment online. This ensures we can give your device our full attention and reduces your waiting time. Booking also helps us confirm that we have the necessary parts in stock for your specific repair.

Pricing & Payment

How much will my repair cost?

The cost of a repair depends on the device and the extent of the damage. We offer free, no-obligation diagnostics and quotes. You can contact us with your device model and issue for an estimated cost, or bring it in for a precise quote.

Is my data safe during repair?

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. We only access your device as necessary for the repair and never view or copy personal data without your explicit permission. For data recovery services, we use secure, encrypted methods to protect your information. We recommend backing up your data before any repair, as we cannot guarantee data preservation in all cases.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), and contactless payments. For larger repairs, we may require payment upfront, but we always discuss this with you first. We do not accept checks or cryptocurrency at this time.

Visiting Us

What should I bring for a repair?

Please bring your device, any accessories (chargers, cases), and proof of purchase if available. If you're dropping off a device, ensure it's charged and unlocked. For laptops, bring the power adapter. We'll provide you with a repair ticket and keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you offer pickup and delivery services?

Currently, we do not offer pickup and delivery services. All repairs must be brought to our shop in Acton Vale. However, we are located conveniently and have free parking available. If you're unable to visit, we can recommend trusted courier services for shipping, though we recommend this only for simple repairs.

What if my device can't be repaired?

In rare cases, a device may be beyond repair due to extensive damage. We'll always inform you upfront if this is the case and provide options like data recovery or device replacement recommendations. There are no charges for diagnostics, and we'll only proceed with repairs after your approval.
